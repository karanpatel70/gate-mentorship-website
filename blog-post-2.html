How I Improved My GATE Score
in My Second Attempt

✍️ By Karan Patel 📅 March 2026 ⏱️ 5 min read 🏷️ GATE CS Strategy
After my first GATE attempt, I realized that hard work alone isn't enough — you need the right strategy. In my second attempt, I made a few key changes that significantly improved my score. Here's exactly what I did differently.
01
Strategy #1

Focusing on High-Scoring Subjects

This time, I prioritized subjects like Engineering Mathematics, Discrete Mathematics, and other high-weight topics. Instead of avoiding them, I worked on building strong fundamentals and practiced them regularly.

These subjects are not only highly scoring but also help strengthen problem-solving skills across other topics.

🎯 Key Takeaway: Smart preparation starts with smart subject selection. Know where the marks are.
// strategy 02
02
Strategy #2

Structured Revision Strategy

One major change I made was implementing a proper revision plan. I didn't just study — I revised consistently. My approach was systematic:

  • Weekly revision of all studied topics
  • Short notes for quick concept recall
  • Multiple revision cycles before the exam

This helped me retain concepts for longer and improved my confidence during problem-solving.

🔁 Key Takeaway: Revision is what separates average preparation from top performance.
// strategy 03
03
Strategy #3

Deep Analysis of PYQs

Previous Year Questions (PYQs) became the core of my preparation. Instead of just solving them, I started analyzing them deeply. I focused on:

  • Understanding how questions are framed
  • Identifying repeated concepts and patterns
  • Practicing multiple variations of the same question

This gave me a clear idea of the exam pattern and made me more comfortable with tricky questions.

📚 Key Takeaway: PYQs are not just practice — they are the blueprint of GATE.
// strategy 04
04
Strategy #4

Taking Mock Tests Seriously

Mock tests played a huge role in my improvement. I treated every mock like the actual exam. From mocks, I learned:

  • Time management under real exam pressure
  • Maintaining accuracy when stressed
  • Identifying weak areas for targeted improvement

After each test, I analyzed my mistakes and worked on them before the next one.

📊 Key Takeaway: Mocks turn preparation into real exam readiness.

✅ Final Thoughts

My second attempt was not about studying more — it was about studying smarter. If you apply these strategies, you can see a major improvement in your GATE score too.

  • ✓ Prioritize high-scoring subjects
  • ✓ Follow a structured revision plan
  • ✓ Practice and analyze PYQs deeply
  • ✓ Take mock tests seriously

// Stay consistent. Trust the process. Results will follow. 🚀
